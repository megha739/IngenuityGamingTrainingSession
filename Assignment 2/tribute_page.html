    Personal life

      20 - june -1958 - born to a Santali family in a village of Odisha

    Early life

      1979 - graduated from Rama Devi Women's University, Bhubaneswar with a BA degree.

      1994 - Started teaching at Aurobindo Integral Education Center, Rairangpur, Odisha

    Political career

      1997 - elected as the councillor of the Rairangpur Nagar Panchayatas an independent candidate from a reserved seat for women.
      Joined Bharatiya janta party
      2000 - 2009 won the Odisha Legislative Assembly election from Rairangpur Assembly constituency and served two terms in the Odisha Legislative Assembly
